# Market Research Workflow

Before product ideation, the BA (Business Analyst) role leads competitive analysis.

## Phase 1: Problem Space

1. What industry or market is the feature solving for?
2. What are the major friction points of the current alternatives?

## Phase 2: Competitor Mapping

Identify 2-3 direct competitors and 1 indirect competitor. What are their implementation strengths and weaknesses?

## Phase 3: Unique Differentiator

Define the "DevForge Advantage"—how will this feature beat the current market solutions? Provide a conclusion summarizing what we should build as a competitive necessity, and what we should cut.
