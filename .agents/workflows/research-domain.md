# Domain Research Workflow

To ensure accurate technical vernacular and industry compliance.

## Phase 1: Subject Matter Terminology

Identify the specific jargon and acronyms used in this domain (e.g., if healthcare: HIPAA, PHI. If fintech: KYC, AML).

## Phase 2: Standard Compliance

Identify any regulations or universally accepted standards (ISO, WCAG, GDPR).

## Phase 3: Process Modeling

Map the core business objects. What is a "User" vs an "Account" vs a "Subscription" in this specific industry domain? Define this taxonomy for the architecture team.
